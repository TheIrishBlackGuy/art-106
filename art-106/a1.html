Assignment 1

Human/Machine Collaboration

This assignment was asking the question as to how we, as artists, could interact and perform with an 'everday' machine. What kind of performance would we doing to make it feel as though the machine itself had a degree of autonomy and was not just a tool being used by the artist.
This assignment had 3 parts to it.
-- Create a diagram that has details of how both the artist and the machine function as a team,
-- Have a 'team photo' with your partner, something that could be the cover of you playbill,
and
-- Have an artistic critque of your performance. They can describe and project how they feel about the project.
