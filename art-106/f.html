ART 106 : Final

Binary reading device

The very original idea I had was to just make a binary counter that had a few tricks of control to it. After a conversation with professor, it was mentioned how the piece is not exactly performative if it is just viewed as a device that has a fixed purpose. I should be able to further interact with it and it provide an output or perform something based on my input. Professor mentioned how it would be interesting if I could translate the binary to ASCII alphabet to then have the device spell something so that the binary input doesn't just stop there but continues on to perform an action.
