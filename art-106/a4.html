ART 106 : Serial Perform

This assignment was a continuation of the prior assignment we had been working on. From the prior assignment, we had the front cover of this assignment. Now we were to build a enclosure to accompany the top. So rather than just the independent lid that we have, we are building something to hold all of our wires, the pico, and the breadboard.
Most of the hardware was now done. So in terms of wiring, I had to better organize wires to make them fit in the planned enclosure so that they definitely did not interfere with each other. Previously, the wires would have been accessible to me but if they are now being contained in a body, I have to confirm the components are comfortably locked in place.
