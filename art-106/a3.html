ART 106 : Micro Perform

We were to use all of our new components to make an interactive board that would be the precursor to the next project. Initially working on with the pico was difficult with trying to understand the differences and purposes of the hardware versus software pins. Once I understood that a bit better, It became easier to manipulate the pins and wires and that seemed to make things a bit easier for me to work in comparison to the original layout that was presented in class.

This assignment would be my first interaction with a laser cutter. Being unfamiliar with illustrator and having made the first reference image in MSpaint, I asked classmates for help. So that kind of resulted in them fixing my Illustrator file and cutting out the board itself.
